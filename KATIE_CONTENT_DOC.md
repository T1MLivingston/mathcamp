## What Is Math Camp Hub?

Math Camp Hub is an interactive summer learning website for students in **Kindergarten through 5th Grade**. It is designed to feel like a summer camp adventure — not a school assignment. Students log in with their email, pick their grade level, and are assigned a camp mascot character. They then work through math activities, take quizzes, collect badges, and download printable worksheets.

The site lives at: **https://t1mlivingston.github.io/mathcamp/**

---

## How the Site Works (Quick Tour)

| Section | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Hero / Homepage** | Welcome screen with the Math Camp banner, grade selector, and camper profile |
| **Pick Your Grade** | Six clickable grade cards (K–5), each with a mascot animal |
| **Activities** | Each grade has 6 activity slots — links, games, or missions for that grade |
| **Quiz** | 10 questions per quiz, drawn from a bank of 50 per activity (so it's different each time) |
| **Printables** | Downloadable PDF worksheets tied to each activity |
| **Badges** | Students earn 16 badges by completing activities, logging in daily, and hitting milestones |
| **Dashboard** | Tracks streak, points, activities completed, and check-ins |

## Badge List (Already Built — For Reference)

Students earn these automatically based on their activity:

| Badge | How to Earn |
|---|---|
| First Camper | Log in for the first time |
| Daily Explorer | Complete first check-in |
| Early Bird | Check in 3 days in a row |
| Five Day Streak | Check in 5 days in a row |
| Ten Day Streak | Check in 10 days in a row |
| Number Ninja | Complete 5 activities |
| Super Star | Complete 10 activities |
| Fraction Master | Complete a fraction activity |
| Math Star | Earn 100 points |
| Point Collector | Earn 250 points |
| High Scorer | Earn 500 points |
| Trail Blazer | Complete all activities in a grade |
| Comeback Kid | Complete the same activity twice |
| Super Repeater | Complete the same activity 5 times |
| Math Legend | Complete the same activity 10 times |
| Camp Champion | Earn 10 badges |
